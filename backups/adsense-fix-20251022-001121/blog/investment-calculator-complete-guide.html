Investment Calculator Complete Guide

SIP vs Lump Sum, CAGR, XIRR, and compound growth explained with quick visuals and calculators.

October 2025 · 10 min read

Core Formulas (At a Glance)

Compound (Lump Sum)

FV = PV × (1 + r)^n
PV: principal, r: rate/period, n: periods

SIP (Recurring)

FV = P × [((1+r)^n − 1)/r] × (1+r)
P: monthly invest, r: monthly rate

CAGR

CAGR = (FV/PV)^(1/t) − 1
t in years

SIP vs Lump Sum: When to Use What?

SIP

  • Great for salaried cashflows
  • Rupee-cost averaging
  • High discipline, low timing risk

Lump Sum

  • Windfall or bonus
  • Use STP to average in
  • Higher timing risk

Quick Examples

Lump Sum: PV ₹2,00,000, r 12% p.a., n 10y → FV ≈ ₹6,21,000
SIP: ₹10,000/mo, r 12% p.a., n 10y → FV ≈ ₹23L (₹12L invest)
