Compound Interest Excel Guide

FV, PMT, and custom formulas with monthly contributions and daily compounding examples.

Excel Formulas

Lump Sum

=FV(rate,nper,0,-pv)
rate: per period, nper: periods, pv: principal

With Monthly PMT

=FV(rate,nper,-pmt,-pv)
pmt: monthly payment, pv: initial

Daily Compounding

=PV*(1+rate/365)^(365*years)

Excel Setup Examples

Monthly SIP in Excel

  • A1: Principal, A2: Monthly PMT
  • B1: Annual Rate, B2: Years
  • C1: =FV(B1/12,B2*12,-A2,-A1)

Daily Compound Interest

  • Use 365 days/year for daily compounding
  • Rate/365 for daily rate
  • 365*years for total periods
