How to Measure Your Ring Size

  1. Wrap a string around your finger
  2. Mark where the string overlaps
  3. Measure the string length in millimeters
  4. Use this as circumference in the converter

Ring Sizing Tips

  • Measure your ring size at the end of the day when fingers are largest
  • Consider the width of the band - wider bands need larger sizes
  • Temperature affects finger size - avoid measuring when very hot or cold
  • If between sizes, choose the larger size for comfort
