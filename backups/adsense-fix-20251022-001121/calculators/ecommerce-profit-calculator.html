How to Use

  • Enter all your product-related costs including manufacturing, shipping, and marketing
  • Add platform fees (Amazon, Flipkart commission) and other miscellaneous costs
  • Set your desired selling price and target monthly quantity
  • Click "Calculate Profit" to see detailed profit analysis and ROI
  • Use the cost breakdown to optimize your pricing strategy

Key Metrics Explained

  • Profit Margin: (Selling Price - Total Cost) / Selling Price × 100
  • ROI: (Profit per Unit / Total Cost) × 100
  • Break-even Price: Minimum selling price to cover all costs
  • Monthly Projections: Based on your target quantity
  • Cost Breakdown: Visual representation of cost distribution

Optimization Tips

  • Aim for a profit margin of at least 20-30% for sustainable business
  • Consider bulk purchasing to reduce per-unit product costs
  • Negotiate better rates with shipping and packaging suppliers
  • Optimize marketing spend for better ROI per customer acquisition
  • Regularly review and adjust pricing based on market conditions
