Quick Conversions

Conversion Formulas

From Celsius (°C)

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32
K = °C + 273.15
°R = (°C + 273.15) × 9/5

From Fahrenheit (°F)

°C = (°F - 32) × 5/9
K = (°F - 32) × 5/9 + 273.15
°R = °F + 459.67

From Kelvin (K)

°C = K - 273.15
°F = (K - 273.15) × 9/5 + 32
°R = K × 9/5

Temperature Scale Reference

Celsius: Water freezes at 0°C, boils at 100°C
Fahrenheit: Water freezes at 32°F, boils at 212°F
Kelvin: Absolute zero at 0K (-273.15°C)
Rankine: Absolute zero at 0°R (-459.67°F)
