💡 EMI Tips for Indian Borrowers:

  • Home loans typically have lower interest rates (7-9%) compared to personal loans (10-24%)
  • Prepayment can significantly reduce your total interest outgo
  • Consider loan insurance to protect your family
  • Tax benefits: Home loan principal (Section 80C) and interest (Section 24) are tax deductible
  • Check for processing fees, typically 0.5-1% of loan amount
  • Maintain a good CIBIL score (750+) for better interest rates
