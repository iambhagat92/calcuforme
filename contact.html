Customer Support & Information

At Calculate For Me, we're committed to providing exceptional support and continuously improving our calculators based on user feedback. Here's how we can help you and how you can reach us.

What We Can Help You With

  • Technical Issues: Calculator not working properly, display problems, or functionality questions
  • Feature Requests: Suggestions for new calculators or improvements to existing ones
  • Accuracy Questions: Clarification about formulas, methods, or calculation results
  • General Inquiries: Questions about our service, privacy policy, or terms of use
  • Bug Reports: Issues with calculator performance or unexpected results
  • Educational Content: Requests for explanations or learning resources

Response Time & Support Hours

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. While we don't have fixed support hours, we typically respond fastest during:

  • Weekdays: Monday through Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST
  • Weekend Response: May take slightly longer, but we check messages regularly
  • Priority Issues: Critical bugs or widespread problems are addressed immediately

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your calculators free?

Yes! All our calculators are completely free to use with no registration required. We believe everyone should have access to accurate calculation tools.

Can I suggest new calculators?

Absolutely! We love hearing suggestions for new calculators. Contact us with your ideas and we'll consider adding them based on user demand and usefulness.

How accurate are the calculations?

Our calculators use industry-standard formulas and are thoroughly tested for accuracy. However, for important financial or health decisions, please consult professionals.

Do you store my data?

No, we prioritize your privacy. All calculations are performed locally in your browser, and we don't store any personal calculation data on our servers.

Can I use calculators on mobile?

Yes! All our calculators are fully responsive and work perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers with any modern web browser.

How often do you update rates?

We regularly update tax rates, interest rates, and other time-sensitive data. Financial calculators are updated at least quarterly or when significant changes occur.
