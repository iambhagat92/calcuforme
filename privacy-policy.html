Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

At CalcuForMe, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and calculators. The short version: We don't collect, store, or share your personal data or calculation inputs.

1. Information We Don't Collect

CalcuForMe is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Your calculation inputs or results
  • Financial data or sensitive information
  • Location data beyond general geographic region
  • Login credentials (we don't have user accounts)
  • Cookies for tracking purposes

All calculations are performed in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.

2. Information We May Collect

We may collect limited, non-personal information for website improvement:

Analytics Data

  • Page views and visit duration (anonymous)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Referring website
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Which calculators are most popular

Note: If we use analytics services like Google Analytics, this data is anonymized and aggregated. It helps us understand which calculators are useful and how to improve our service.

3. How We Use Information

Any anonymous data we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Understand which calculators are most valuable
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Decide which new calculators to add
  • Optimize for different devices and browsers

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal cookies and browser storage:

Essential Cookies

We may use essential cookies for basic website functionality (e.g., remembering your preferences like dark mode if we implement it).

Analytics Cookies

If we use analytics services, they may set cookies to track anonymous usage. You can disable these in your browser settings.

Local Storage

Some calculators may save your preferences (like unit selection) locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared anytime.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

Google Analytics (if implemented)

For anonymous usage statistics. See Google's Privacy Policy.

CDN Services (Font Awesome)

We load icons from CDNs for fast performance. These services may log basic request information.

Hosting Provider

Our hosting provider may collect server logs (IP addresses, access times) for security and performance purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect our website:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Regular security updates to our website
  • No database storage of user calculations
  • Client-side processing means your data stays on your device

7. Children's Privacy

CalcuForMe is safe for all ages. We don't knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Our calculators can be used by students, parents, and educators without any data collection concerns.

8. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no personal information to:

  • Request access to
  • Request deletion of
  • Request correction of
  • Request portability of

You can clear your browser's cookies and local storage at any time to remove any calculator preferences stored locally.

9. International Users

CalcuForMe is accessible worldwide. Since we don't collect personal data, there are no international data transfer concerns. Our website is hosted on servers that may be located in various countries, but your calculation data never leaves your browser.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Significant changes will be highlighted on our homepage for 30 days after implementation.
