How to Measure Your Face

1
Forehead Width: Measure across your forehead at the widest point, typically from hairline to hairline above your eyebrows.
2
Cheekbone Width: Measure from the outer edge of one cheekbone to the other at the widest point (usually just below your eyes).
3
Jawline Width: Measure from the tip of your chin straight up to below your ear, then multiply by 2.
4
Face Length: Measure from the center of your hairline straight down to the tip of your chin.

Pro Tip: Use a flexible measuring tape or string, and measure in centimeters or inches. Take a selfie with your hair pulled back for easier measurement.
