Tax Benefits

  • Section 80C: EPF contributions up to ₹1.5 lakh are tax deductible
  • Tax-free Interest: EPF interest is tax-free if withdrawn after 5 years
  • EEE Status: Exempt-Exempt-Exempt for tax purposes
  • Partial Withdrawal: Tax-free for specific purposes like home purchase
