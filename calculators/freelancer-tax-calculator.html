Tax Tips for Freelancers

  • Track Everything: Keep detailed records of all income and expenses
  • Quarterly Payments: Pay estimated taxes quarterly to avoid penalties
  • Deductible Expenses: Home office, equipment, software, internet, phone
  • Retirement: Solo 401(k) or SEP IRA can reduce taxable income significantly
  • Health Insurance: Self-employed health insurance is fully deductible
  • Professional Help: Consider hiring a CPA for complex situations
