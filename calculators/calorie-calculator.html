About Calorie Calculation

This calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation to estimate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), then multiplies it by your activity level to get your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE).

Understanding the Results:

  • BMR: Calories burned at rest, maintaining basic bodily functions
  • TDEE: Total calories burned daily including activity
  • Weight Loss: 500 calorie deficit = ~1 lb weight loss per week
  • Weight Gain: 500 calorie surplus = ~1 lb weight gain per week
