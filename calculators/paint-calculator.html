About Paint Coverage

This paint calculator helps you estimate the amount of paint needed for your room or project. It calculates the total wall area, subtracts doors and windows, and accounts for multiple coats.

Standard Coverage Rates:

  • Metric: ~10 square meters per liter
  • Imperial: ~350 square feet per gallon
  • Typical Door: 2 m² or ~20 sq ft
  • Typical Window: 1.5 m² or ~15 sq ft

Note: Actual coverage may vary based on paint type, surface texture, and application method. Always purchase slightly more paint than calculated to account for variations and touch-ups.
