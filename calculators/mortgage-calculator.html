How Mortgage Payments are Calculated

Your monthly mortgage payment consists of several components:

  • Principal & Interest (P&I): Calculated using the standard amortization formula
  • Property Tax: Annual property tax divided by 12 months
  • Home Insurance: Annual insurance premium divided by 12 months
  • HOA Fees: Monthly homeowners association fees
  • PMI: Private Mortgage Insurance, typically required if down payment is less than 20%
M = P × [r(1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n-1]

Where: M = Monthly payment, P = Principal (loan amount), r = Monthly interest rate, n = Number of payments
