Understanding EPF Calculation

How EPF Interest is Calculated?

EPF interest is calculated monthly but credited annually. The interest is computed on the opening balance of each month plus the contributions made.

Employer's Contribution Split

Out of 12% employer contribution: 3.67% goes to EPF and 8.33% to EPS (Pension Scheme). EPS contribution is capped at wage ceiling of â‚¹15,000.

Tax Benefits

EPF enjoys EEE tax status - Exempt at investment, accumulation, and withdrawal stages (after 5 years of service).
