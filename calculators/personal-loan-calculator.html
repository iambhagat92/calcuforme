Personal Loan FAQs

What is the maximum personal loan amount I can get?

Most banks in India offer personal loans up to ₹40-50 lakhs, depending on your income and credit profile. Typically, the loan amount is 10-15 times your monthly salary.

How is personal loan EMI calculated?

EMI is calculated using the formula: EMI = [P x R x (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N-1], where P is principal, R is monthly interest rate, and N is tenure in months.

Can I prepay my personal loan?

Yes, most banks allow prepayment after 6-12 EMIs. Some banks charge 2-4% prepayment penalty, while others offer free prepayment.
