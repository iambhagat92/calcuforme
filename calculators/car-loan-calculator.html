üí° Car Loan Tips & Guide

Get Best Interest Rates

  • Compare rates from multiple banks
  • Maintain credit score above 750
  • Consider dealer financing offers
  • Negotiate with existing bank relationship

Down Payment Strategy

  • Pay 15-20% down payment minimum
  • Higher down payment = lower EMI
  • Reduces total interest cost
  • Improves loan approval chances

Choose Right Tenure

  • Shorter tenure = lower total interest
  • Longer tenure = lower EMI
  • Max: 7 years new, 5 years used
  • Balance EMI with total cost

Required Documents

  • Identity & Address proof
  • Income proof (3 months salary)
  • Bank statements (6 months)
  • Car quotation/invoice

‚ùì Car Loan FAQs

What is the maximum car loan amount I can get?

Most banks offer car loans up to ‚Çπ50 lakhs. The exact amount depends on your income, typically 4-5 times your monthly salary. For luxury cars, some banks offer higher amounts.

What's the difference between new and used car loan rates?

New car loans have lower interest rates (7.5-12%) compared to used car loans (9-15%). New cars also qualify for higher funding percentage and longer repayment tenure.

Can I prepay my car loan without penalty?

Most banks allow prepayment after 6-12 EMI payments. Some charge 2-5% prepayment penalty, while others offer free prepayment. Check with your lender before taking the loan.
