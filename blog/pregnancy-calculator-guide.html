Pregnancy Calculator Guide

By convention, pregnancy dating starts from the Last Menstrual Period (LMP). The estimated due date (EDD) is 280 days (40 weeks) after LMP (Naegele’s rule).

EDD = LMP + 280 days Gestational Age (weeks) = floor((Today − LMP) / 7) Trimester: 1st (<13w), 2nd (13–27w), 3rd (≥28w)

Example: LMP = 1 Jan → EDD ≈ 8 Oct; if today is 15 Apr, GA ≈ 14 weeks.

Note: Actual due date varies; ultrasound can refine dating.
