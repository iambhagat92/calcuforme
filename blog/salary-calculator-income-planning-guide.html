CTC vs In-hand

  • CTC includes employer costs (EPF, gratuity, insurance).
  • In-hand = Earnings − Deductions (tax, EPF, PT, etc.).
  • Pick old vs new tax regime based on deductions (HRA, 80C, 80D).
