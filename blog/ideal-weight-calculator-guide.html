Ideal Weight Calculator Guide

Several simple formulas estimate Ideal Body Weight (IBW). They’re references—not medical prescriptions. Compare multiple methods to get a sensible range.

Devine (M): 50 + 2.3 × (inches over 60) Devine (F): 45.5 + 2.3 × (inches over 60) Robinson (M): 52 + 1.9 × x | (F): 49 + 1.7 × x Miller (M): 56.2 + 1.41 × x | (F): 53.1 + 1.36 × x Hamwi (M): 48 + 2.7 × x | (F): 45.5 + 2.2 × x

Example (Female, 165 cm ≈ 65 in, x = 5): Devine ≈ 57.0 kg, Robinson ≈ 57.5 kg, Miller ≈ 59.9 kg, Hamwi ≈ 56.5 kg. An average near ~57.7 kg.

Tip: Use IBW with BMI & body fat to build a complete picture.
