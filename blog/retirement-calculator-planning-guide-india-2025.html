Retirement Calculator India 2025 - Complete Retirement Planning Guide

By Calculate For Me Team October 25, 2025
16 min read
Retirement Planning

Expert Summary: Planning for retirement is crucial for financial security in your golden years. This comprehensive guide explains how to use a retirement calculator, calculate your retirement corpus, understand inflation impact, and plan effectively with NPS, PPF, EPF, and other investment options in India.

Key Takeaways

  • ✓Need 25-30x your annual expenses for retirement
  • ✓Start retirement planning in your 20s to maximize compound interest
  • ✓Save 15-20% of gross income for retirement
  • ✓Inflation reduces purchasing power by 50% every 12 years at 6%
  • ✓NPS offers extra ₹50,000 tax deduction under Section 80CCD(1B)
  • ✓Diversify across NPS, PPF, EPF, and equity mutual funds
