❓ FAQ - Instant Personal Loans

How fast can I get a personal loan approved?

With good credit score and proper documents, you can get approval within 5-15 minutes. Disbursal happens within 24 hours for most lenders.

What is the minimum credit score for instant loans?

Most instant loan providers require a minimum credit score of 650-700. Some fintech apps accept scores as low as 600 but with higher interest rates.

Are instant loans safe?

Yes, if you use RBI-regulated banks and NBFCs. Avoid unregistered apps and always verify the lender's credentials before applying.
