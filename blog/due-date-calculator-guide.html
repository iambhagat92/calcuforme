Due Date Calculator Guide (EDD)

The standard dating method uses LMP + 280 days. If you know your conception date, estimate LMP as conception − 14 days (typical cycle), then add 280 days for EDD.

EDD = LMP + 280 days If conception is known: LMP ≈ Conception − 14 days → EDD = LMP + 280

Example: LMP 1 Jan → EDD ≈ 8 Oct.
